Be a Bridge Over



1.Golden Bridge

Amid the foggy hills near Da Nang, Vietnam sits the 150-meters-long 'Golden Bridge'. A breath taking piece design by TA Landscape Architecture.





2.Juscelino Kubitschek Bridge

Inagurated in December 15,2002 and named after former president, the father of modern Brazil, Juscelino Kubitschek, the bridge crosses Lake Paranoa and is accessible to bicyclists and skaters too. The inspiration for such creation came from Architect and Brasilia's striking sunset.





3.Nescio Bridge

Designed by London-based Wilkinson Eyre. This structure was built over the Amsterdam-Rhine Canal.





4.Banpo Bridge

Connecting the districts of Seocho and Yongsan, sits the Banpo Bridge (the world's longest bridge fountain). Illuminated by 200 LED lights and in synchronization with music the fountain jets water in the air.





5.Aiola Island Bridge

This modern pedestrian bridge that sits between two river banks, holds a popular restaurant inside it. Design by architect Vito Acconci.
